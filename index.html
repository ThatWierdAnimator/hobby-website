Your Only Move is
HUSTLE

Background:

Your Only Move is Hustle is a fighting game developed by Ivy Sly in 2023. What sets it apart is that it is turn based. Whenever a player can input a new move, the game will pause and let them think before choosing a move. Instead of having to react and learn inputs, YOMI Hustle players focus solely on prediction and poking skills. When the game is finished, it plays back in real time, without pauses, and looks real cool.

Characters:

The characters currently in the game are:

  • Ninja
  • Cowboy
  • Wizard
  • Robot
  • Mutant
Every charcter is very unique, and has something that sets it apart from the crowd. Not every character is made equal, however, so some have a steeper learning curve than others. The most beginner friendly to complicated chracters are as follows:
  1. Cowboy
  2. Ninja
  3. Mutant
  4. Robot
  5. Wizard

Cowboy:

The Cowboy is a cowboy with a sword, lasso, revolver, and teleport at his disposal. Cowboy is the first character new players should pick up, as they have the safest movement option in the game, teleport. Teleport moves very quickly, only needing only a few frames to use, and can move a player very far in a controlled and unpredictable way. Since this is almost immediate, Cowboy players can use this to escape combos or to keep their combos going. Their normals are very fast and easy to understand, making him the best character to pick up first.

Conclusion:

In conlusion, while YOMI Hustle may be complicated at first, it is a very unique take on the fighting game genre, and extremely fun once you know your stuff. It is my personal favorite fighting game, and deserves the Most Banger Award for 2024.
